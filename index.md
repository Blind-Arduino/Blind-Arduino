## Welcome to Blind Arduino

This is a placeholder for a much more exciting and complete Blind Arduino web site currently under development.

The new site will include tutorials, designs for accessible tools and equipment, product recommendations, links to other resources, and much more.

## Stay Connected

To keep in touch with the Blind Arduino community, sign up for our low-traffic Blind Arduino email list at groups.io. Just send a blank email to [babamm+subscribe@groups.io](mailto:babamm+subscribe@groups.io), then check your email and reply to the groups.io email to confirm.
